    About Brightaire Property Services

    Contact Us Now   ➜

    The locally owned Brightaire Property Services has been established on the Sunshine Coast for over 20 years. The high standard service delivery has been instilled in our work practices for the same period and has been at the core of the business. We take homage to previous management for their dedication to the business and establishing the foundations as a leader in quality and customer service.

    Proudly family owned and operated by Sunshine Coast locals the DeCourcy family, Brightaire Property Services are a team of professional workers and great characters who you can always rely on. You won’t find a more professional, down to earth and determined team to deliver a great result on every job without compromise.

    Rest assured that all Brightaire Property Group staff are not only professionally trained, but also trustworthy people with full police checks, so you know that the people who are entering your property are reliable in more ways than one.

    Facts about Noddy

    1. Born in Liverpool England

    2. Liverpool supporter through and through

    3. Moved to Australia at 14 years old

    4. Started playing football on the Sunshine Coast in 1972, playing for Woombye (10 years), then Nambour Reds, which his parents started that Club, also did 6-month trial for Blackpool Football Club in England, and been at Buderim Wanderers Football Club since 1985 – the year the senior team won every competition that they played in, and still a strong supporter of the Club today.
